🧹 Discord Message Wiper

Delete your own messages from a server you're in — locally and privately.

⚠️ Read this before you start

  • This tool acts as your own account using your account token. Automating a user account is against Discord's Terms of Service and could, in theory, get your account actioned. Use at your own risk.
  • It only deletes messages you wrote. It cannot touch other people's messages.
  • Deletion is permanent. There is no undo. Always run a Dry run first.
  • Your token is kept in this browser tab only and is sent solely to the local proxy that forwards it to Discord. It is never logged or stored on a server.
  • Never share your token with anyone. It grants full access to your account.

1. Connect your account

  1. Open Discord in your browser (not the desktop app) and log in.
  2. Press F12 to open Developer Tools, then go to the Network tab.
  3. Send any message or switch channels so requests appear.
  4. Click a request to discord.com/api, open Headers, and find the authorization request header. That value is your token.

Treat your token like a password. This tool runs locally and never transmits it anywhere except Discord (via the local proxy).
